# HelixMind BioAI

Artificial Intelligence for the Next Generation of Cellular Immunotherapy.

HelixMind BioAI is an independent research initiative focused on exploring how artificial intelligence can support the study of cancer biology and cellular immunotherapy, particularly CAR-T cell therapies.

This project aims to investigate computational approaches that can help analyze biological complexity, identify potential therapeutic targets, and explore large-scale genomic and immunological datasets.

---

## Vision

To explore the intersection between artificial intelligence and biological discovery in order to contribute to the future of precision medicine and cellular therapies.

---

## Mission

Develop computational frameworks capable of assisting biological research through:

- bioinformatics
- machine learning
- genomic data analysis
- computational immunology
- biological sequence modeling

---

## Core Project

### CAR-T Intelligence Engine

The first conceptual system being developed within HelixMind BioAI is the **CAR-T Intelligence Engine**.

The objective of this framework is to explore computational strategies for:

- biological sequence analysis
- tumor antigen exploration
- genomic data interpretation
- immunological pattern discovery
- AI-assisted biological research

This project is intended as a **research-oriented computational framework**, not a clinical tool.

---

## Research Areas

HelixMind BioAI focuses on the intersection of several scientific and computational fields:

- Cancer Biology

- Bioinformatics
