# HelixMind BioAI – 180 Day Development Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the first phase of development for the HelixMind BioAI research initiative.

The project is organized into several stages:

## Phase 1 – Biological Foundations
Understanding cancer biology, tumor immunology, and cellular therapy.

## Phase 2 – Bioinformatics
Learning how biological sequences, genomic data, and molecular information are represented computationally.

## Phase 3 – Machine Learning for Biology
Exploring machine learning methods applied to biological datasets.

## Phase 4 – CAR-T Intelligence Engine
Designing and developing a conceptual computational framework for exploring biological data related to CAR-T therapy.

## Phase 5 – Experiments
Implementing computational experiments and documenting results.

The progress of this roadmap will be documented through daily research logs over a 180-day period.
