# Principles

HelixMind BioAI is guided by the following principles:

## Open Research

All research development will be documented and shared publicly whenever possible.

## Scientific Integrity

The project respects the principles of scientific rigor, transparency, and reproducibility.

## Interdisciplinary Exploration

Advances in biological discovery often occur at the intersection of multiple disciplines. HelixMind BioAI integrates knowledge from computer science, biology, and data science.

## Ethical Responsibility

This project is intended for research and educational exploration. It does not provide medical advice, diagnostic tools, or clinical recommendations.

## Long-Term Curiosity

Understanding biological systems is a long-term challenge. HelixMind BioAI embraces continuous learning and exploration.
